Pizza

How to make Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 500 g Flour
  • 20 slices Pepperoni
  • 1 Pack of Cheese
  • 1 can of Tomato Sauce

Directions:

  1. Mix flour with water
  2. Knead the dough and use a roller pin to flatten the dough
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  4. Spread Cheese on the dough
  5. Add pepperoni on the dough
  6. Bake for 20 mins at 400 F
  7. After 20 mins take out pizza, leave to cool for 5 mins and enjoy

Banana Bread

How to make Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 500 g Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Banana
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  • Milk

Directions:

  1. Mix Sugar and Eggs until fluffy
  2. Add Flour litle by little
  3. Add the banana
  4. Add milk if needed
  5. Bake for 20 mins at 400 F
  6. After 20 mins take out Banana Bread, leave to cool for 5 mins and enjoy
